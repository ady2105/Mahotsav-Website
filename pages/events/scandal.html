Scandal

Create scandalous & hilarious news headlines and fulfill your fantasy of becoming an editor for a day! Come and enjoy one of the funniest events!

Relevant material will be provided to you.

  • It is an individual event.
  • Time limit of 1 hr.
  • No restriction on headlines.
  • All artwork is preferred to be original.
  • Participants will have to create an interesting headline using newspaper cuttings.
  • Each participant will get 5-6 minutes to explain their artwork.
  • Judging criteria: work, discipline, cleanliness, and interpretation.
  • Decision by judges are final and abiding.
  • The participant with any indiscipline activities will be disqualified.
  • For any queries, contact the team coordinator.
