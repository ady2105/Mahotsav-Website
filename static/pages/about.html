About Us

The Photography and Fine Arts Sub-Council (PFA) functions under the Council of Student Activity (CSA) at the Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering and Technology, Jhansi, and serves as a dynamic hub for artistic expression and creativity. The sub-council actively promotes fine arts and hobbies through activities such as sketching, collage, face painting, movie-making, photography, and various other creative disciplines. PFA has the privilege of organizing MAHOTSAV, a 5–7 day annual art festival recognized as one of the largest art festivals in the region. In addition, PFA enthusiastically celebrates Dussehra every year with cultural events and vibrant Dandiya Nights, fostering tradition and student participation. The sub-council operates under the guidance of Dr. Satish Kumar Singh, Officer-in-Charge, with the continued support and inspiration of the Hon’ble Director, Prof. Jitendra Kumar Sharma.
